Eight Years of Transforming Lives

Since 2018, Judah Praise has been the catalyst for extraordinary medical breakthroughs at Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital. Each concert, each donation, each prayer has contributed to a legacy of healing that spans across departments and touches thousands of lives.

Our Journey of Impact

2018

Stroke Monitoring System

Our inaugural project launched with the acquisition of advanced stroke monitoring equipment, establishing the foundation for rapid response stroke care. This system enabled 24/7 monitoring of critical stroke patients.

1,200+ Patients Monitored
85% Recovery Rate

2019

Pediatric Surgery Equipment

Focused on our youngest patients, we provided specialized surgical equipment for children's operations, including micro-surgical instruments and pediatric anesthesia monitors that transformed child surgical outcomes.

800+ Child Surgeries
95% Success Rate

Bilirubin Meters for Newborns

During the challenging COVID-19 year, we pivoted to support newborn care with advanced bilirubin meters, enabling early detection and treatment of jaundice in premature and full-term babies.
