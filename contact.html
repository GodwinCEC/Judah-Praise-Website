Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our work and how to get involved

How can I volunteer with Judah Praise CMF?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including event organization, medical support, fundraising, and administrative tasks. Contact us with your interests and availability, and we'll find the perfect way for you to contribute.

Can organizations partner with Judah Praise CMF?

Absolutely! We actively seek partnerships with healthcare organizations, faith-based groups, corporations, and NGOs. Partner organizations can sponsor specific equipment, co-host events, or provide expertise for our projects.

How do you ensure donation transparency?

We provide quarterly reports detailing fund usage, maintain partnership verification with KATH, and ensure 85% of donations go directly to medical equipment and patient support. All major purchases are documented and verified.

When is the next Judah Praise concert?

Our annual praise and worship concert is typically held in December. Stay connected through our social media channels and contact list for announcements about the 2025 event supporting the Stroke Project 2026.
